My favorite products in Sephora

I have many favorite products in Sephora. My top three products is the Summer Fridays jet lag mask, the Hourglass brow pencil, and the Glossier Cloud Paint.

Jet Lag Mask

This is the Summer Fridays jet lag mask. This is a very thick product. I have eczema and this product does not flare up my eczema. I suggest this product to anyone in need of a thick moisturizer.

Hourglass Brow Pencil

This is the Hourglass brow pencil. This product fills in brows but makes them look natural. I am a redhead so finding products for my brows is hard..

Glossier Cloud Paint

This is the Glossier Cloud Paint. It is a blush that is buildable. You can go for a natural or more dramatic look. It is the perfect blush for anyone who does not know what blush to buy.
