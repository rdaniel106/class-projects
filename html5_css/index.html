Sephoras brands

Sephora carries many well known brands and also their own brand. They carry expensive brands and also viral brands.They carry many brands so customers have a wide selection. They are very strict about what brands they allow in their stores. They carry luxury brands like Chanel, Dior, but have viral brands like Glow Recipe and Rhode. They do keep brands in stores for as long as possible so customers will keep shopping in Sephora but Sephora is constanly looking for new brands.

Sephora likes balance. They want customers to know that their trusty brands are still in Sephora but they also want to incourage customers to branch out to new brands. They also cater to all age groups and have employees there to help you find products for your age group. Sephora has some brands that focus on bold makeup looks while others focus on skincare and simple makeup. Sephora also carries brands that focus on haircare and bodycare. Sephora carries the brand Olaplex, which is a haircare line, and they also carry Sol de Janeiro, which is a body care line. Sephora likes to have a variety!
