More about Sephora

Sephora carries the most viral and loved brands. While they are very expesnive brands, they are the most popular. A brand can get denied several times before having one product in Sephora. Some brands are high end like Chanel and Dior. Other brands are celebrity owned like Rhode and Rare beauty. They try to only bring in clean brands that are cruelty free like Tower 28.

Sephora is not just about big names. They really study brands to protect their store. If a brand gets into a scandal then they will usually take the brand out of the store. They also have bins where you can put old products so you do not waste packaging. This goes back to them trying to be earth friendly. Sephora is a store who really tries to satisfy customers.
